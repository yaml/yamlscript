When I started the Summer of YS blog series on June 1st, I committed to writing
a blog post every day until September 1st (92 days in a row).

Today it's Friday, August 1st, 2025 and today's post is number 62.

It's also the last one!

<!-- more -->


## It was a good run!

OK, I'm not going to stop working on YS or writing about it.

I'm just not going to write a post tomorrow or Sunday.
I don't know when I'll write the next one, but it will be soon enough.

It's not that I don't have plenty to write about.
I need to be spending my time working on YS and making even better things to
tell you about.

I'm also not going to end this post without showing you something cool!


## The YS Alphabet

We've done a few Rosetta Code problems together this summer.

Last weekend I noticed the first letter of the Rosetta Code completed YS tasks
covered about half of the alphabet.

As of today [YS has a completed task for every letter of the alphabet](
https://rosettacode.org/wiki/Category:YAMLScript#mw-pages)!


## Stay Tuned!

If you've been following along, thanks for reading!

I imagine I'll still be writing 1 or 2 posts a week.

And hopefully with less frequency comes more in-depth content.

After you do this for 50+ days in a row, it doesn't feel like a big deal.

Stay YS and I'll see you here soon!
